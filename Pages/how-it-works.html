How it works

Study vocabulary, practise it in context, and follow your progress.

Vocamira combines focused study cards, English definitions, example sentences, two quiz modes, and personal progress tracking. The aim is to help you understand how words are used instead of memorising isolated translations.

01

Study each word in context

Browse vocabulary by level, read all three example sentences, and see the target word highlighted in each sentence. The examples remain visible even when the definition is hidden.

02

Reveal the definition when you are ready

Try to understand the word from its example sentences first, then use the Show definition button to check its English meaning.

03

Build a quiz that matches your session

Choose Definition Quiz or Fill in the Blank, select a difficulty group and category, then set a session length of 10, 20, 30, or 50 questions.

04

Learn from immediate feedback

After every answer, Vocamira shows whether your choice was correct and reveals the correct answer before you continue.

05

Track your learning progress

Signed-in users can view games played, questions answered, accuracy, performance by quiz mode, and the number of learned, learning, and difficult words.

Why English definitions?

Build direct understanding instead of translating first.

English definitions and complete example sentences expose you to useful vocabulary, natural sentence patterns, and the way words appear in dictionaries, lessons, and everyday communication.

Choose how you want to begin.

Study the vocabulary at your own pace or create a quiz and start practising immediately.
