How it works

A focused way to understand and remember English words.

Vocamira teaches vocabulary through English definitions, short quizzes, and immediate feedback. The goal is to help you understand each word in English instead of relying on direct translation.

01

Read one word

Each question focuses on a single English word, keeping the task clear and manageable.

02

Compare English definitions

You choose between four English definitions. This encourages you to think directly in English rather than translate first.

03

Get immediate feedback

After every answer, the correct definition is shown. This helps you correct mistakes while the question is still fresh.

04

Complete a short session

Each game contains ten questions, making it easy to practise without committing to a long lesson.

Why definitions?

Learn meaning through context, not translation.

English definitions expose you to more useful vocabulary and help you recognise how words are explained in real dictionaries, lessons, and conversations.
